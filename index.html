Just listen to that sustain!

Instructions:

  • Click and hold piano keys to play.
  • Press and hold keyboard keys to play.
  • Press spacebar for sustain petal.

Steinway samples are from the University of Iowa

Issues:

  • The samples are 15MB total. Each is playable once it is downloaded.
  • If a key is "stuck" just click it again. If that doesn't work, it's a bug (let me know).
  • If your browser refuses to play ogg files, get a better browser.
  • The number of simultaneous keys you can play is limited by your computer keyboard and potentially your operating system.
  • If you hear choppy sounds when pressing a single key, it is probably a bug in your audio mixer.
  • If you hear choppy sounds when pressing many keys simultaneously, it is probably a limit in your audio mixer, maybe a browser quirk, but I ain't fixin' it.
